Guts Over Fear

Eminem feat. Sia

"Guts Over Fear" is a single by Eminem featuring Australian singer Sia, from Eminem's compilation album SHADYXV, released on August 25, 2014. The song was produced by Emile Haynie and John Hill and was included in the trailer of the film, The Equalizer.
